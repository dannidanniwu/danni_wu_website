Danni Wu, Ph.D.

I'm a biostatistician at Pfizer, where I serve as statistical lead for oncology clinical trials and build Bayesian methods that help teams make earlier, better-calibrated decisions.

Before Pfizer, I was a postdoctoral research associate at Harvard University and earned my Ph.D. in Biostatistics at New York University, where my work on Bayesian adaptive design informed the FDA's revised policy on COVID-19 convalescent plasma.
